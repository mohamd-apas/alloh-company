Services We Offer

AL-LOUH Company Services

Al-Louh Company LTD has evolved into a full service Construction Company – one that is unparalleled in its skill, expertise and diversity.


Al-Louh Company LTD team can develop performance oriented scheduling and detailed phasing to ensure that all of its projects operate efficiently


Al-Louh Company LTD’s engineers routinely examine designs and plans for constructability. The team of engineers may suggest alternate systems known as “Value Engineering” to reduce the time schedule and/or the budget without compromising quality.


Al-Louh Company LTD’s Estimating Department utilizes the latest in estimating software as well as an in-house computerized data bank of materials that include equipment and labor costs, to effectively and competitively quote all types of projects.

Building Construction

We specialize in construction projects for commercial housing, industrial building complexes, warehouses and substations.

Civil Infrastructure

We deliver infrastructure projects in the areas of sewage, storm water
Management, water supply, telephone lines, and electrical ducts including Micro tunneling and CCTV (closed circuit television) ducts.
We also focus on construction and maintenance of water reservoirs, sewage treatment plants and pumping

Roads And Landscaping

We specialize in motorways construction and maintenance, in particular, delivering suburban roads and landscaping
including sub-base, base course and bituminous asphalt, road marking and signage. We are also capable of bridge
maintenance and repair including specialty works such as epoxy injection.

Oil Sector Construction

We are with the participation of Hadeer Al-Ahwer Company worked in oil sector construction of gathering centers, heavy civil foundations, pipe racks including structural fabrication erection and foundations, tank farm, pre-engineered buildings, dikes, ditches, roads, curbs, and parking bays. We also participate in maintenance projects for government oil companies.

Electrical-Mechanical Works

Our electro-mechanical works are related to sewage pumping stations, water treatment plants, street lighting for roads and motorways, firefighting systems, and piping work for oil sector.

AL-louh Co. is a highly-specialized in construction company with core competence in building and construction, civil infrastructure, roads and landscaping. Located in Iraq / Baghdad
