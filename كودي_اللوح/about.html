AL-louh Co. is a highly-specialized in construction company with core competence in building and construction, civil infrastructure, roads and landscaping. Located in Iraq / Baghdad, AL-louh Co. serves both government and private sectors with unparalleled engineering skills, professionalism and strong commitment for each and every project. For over 15 years, AL-Louh Co. has built a reputation as the most valuable construction company in Maysan in particular and in Iraq in general by delivering turnkey construction projects of varying sizes to every part of Iraq. In recent years, AL-Louh Co. has expanded its construction activity in Iraq.

Because of engineering excellence and project management expertise, AL-louh Co. has been labeled as a “Grade I” company for infrastructure work and building by Maysan Municipality and the Central Tendering Committee.

AL-Louh CO.’s primary safety rule for accidents is governed by: Zero Tolerance. This policy not only enhances the safety of work crews, it also has the benefits of reducing injury-related downtime and maintaining reduced rates of materials damage.

HSE Plan

The guidelines for the development of the HSE plan in the building & construction sector was issued and linked to the e-services of the
construction permit to serve the following objectives:

  1. To guide all building & construction sector entities (consultants, contractors & developers) to prepare & develop a health, safety & environment plan for the building construction sites & the provision of an integrated HSE data to assist in the management & performance monitoring of the projects in the normal activities & in case of emergencies.

  2. Explains all the responsibilities entrusted to the consultants, contractors, project managers and HSE team and the role of each of them contributing to benefit to the day to day construction activities.

  3. Introducing the governmental entities relevant to the building & construction sector & simplify the communication with specialists.

  4. Take the appropriate preventive measures to safeguard the health & safety of workers & protect the environment through the application of standards to identify the hazards, assess the risks develop a clear strategy to control these risks.

  5. Adds many advantages to companies & relevant institutions to reduce the costs of accidents, injuries & the insurance premiums & raise the overall staff moral & increase the confidence in their companies.

  6. The guidelines help the HSE personnel in a scientific manner to monitor the HSE performance through the use of the appropriate inspection checklists & accordingly develop the appropriate control measures.
